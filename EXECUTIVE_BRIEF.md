# PVARA HRMS - Executive Brief
**Human Resource Management System**  
*Date: 11 December 2025*

---

## Executive Summary

PVARA HRMS is a comprehensive, cloud-ready Human Resource Management System custom-built to streamline and automate HR operations. The platform delivers enterprise-grade capabilities with modern technology infrastructure, designed to scale with organizational growth while maintaining operational excellence.

---

## Key Capabilities

### Core Modules
The system encompasses **10+ integrated modules** covering the complete employee lifecycle:

- **Employee Management** - Centralized employee database, departmental hierarchy, and organizational structure
- **Payroll Management** - PKR-native salary processing, automated calculations, payslip generation, and CSV bulk upload
- **Leave Management** - Multi-type leave tracking, balance management, and approval workflows
- **Attendance System** - Digital check-in/out, late arrival tracking, and comprehensive reporting
- **Performance Management** - Goal setting, performance appraisals, and 360-degree feedback mechanisms
- **Learning & Development** - Training course management, certification tracking, and skill development
- **Recruitment** - Job posting, applicant tracking, and candidate pipeline management
- **Compliance Management** - Policy documentation, audit trails, and regulatory compliance
- **Analytics Dashboard** - Real-time insights, custom reports, and data-driven decision support
- **Access Control** - Role-based permissions with four distinct user levels

### Role-Based Access Control

**Administrator** - Complete system access, configuration, and settings management  
**HR Manager** - Employee lifecycle management, payroll operations, and compliance oversight  
**Department Manager** - Team management, leave approvals, and performance evaluation  
**Employee** - Self-service portal for personal information, leave requests, and performance tracking

## Technical Infrastructure

**Frontend Architecture**
- React 19.2.1 with Vite 7.2.7 for optimal performance
- Tailwind CSS for responsive, modern interface design
- Progressive Web App (PWA) capabilities

**Backend Architecture**
- Node.js with Express.js framework
- RESTful API architecture for scalability
- JWT-based authentication and authorization

**Database & Deployment**
- MongoDB for flexible, scalable data management
- Vercel cloud infrastructure for 24/7 availability
- Production deployment at: **https://pvara.team**
